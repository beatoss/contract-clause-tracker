Supply Agreement

This Supply Agreement is made between MedParts AG and CarePlus Clinics Ltd.

The supplier will provide medical consumables according to accepted purchase orders.

Either party may terminate this agreement without cause upon 60 days written notice.

<!-- Clause Type: Termination for Convenience -->

The supplier's liability shall be limited to the replacement cost of the affected products.

<!-- Clause Type: Limitation of Liability -->

The customer shall not purchase equivalent products from a direct competitor during the exclusivity period.

<!-- Clause Type: Non-Compete -->

All deliveries must comply with applicable Swiss healthcare regulations.
