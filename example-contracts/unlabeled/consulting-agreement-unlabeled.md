# Consulting Agreement

This Consulting Agreement is entered into between Alpine Strategy AG and Northstar Retail Ltd.

The consultant will provide market research and strategic planning services.

The client may terminate this agreement for convenience by giving 15 days written notice.

The consultant's total liability shall not exceed the fees paid under this agreement during the previous 6 months.

Neither party may disclose proprietary project information without prior written consent.

The consultant shall deliver a final report by the agreed project deadline.
