# Freelance Design Agreement

This Freelance Design Agreement is entered into by Studio Forma and Example Ventures AG.

The designer will create brand assets, presentation templates, and website graphics.

The client may end the project at any time by providing written notice and paying for completed work.

The designer shall not work for a direct competitor of the client for 6 months after the project ends.

The designer's liability for any claim shall be limited to the total fees received for the project.

Final source files will be transferred after payment of all outstanding invoices.
