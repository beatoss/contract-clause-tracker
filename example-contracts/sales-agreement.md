# Sales Agreement

This Sales Agreement is entered into between SecureWare AG and Regional Bank Ltd.

SecureWare AG will deliver 250 encrypted hardware devices to the customer.

The customer shall pay the purchase price within 20 days after receipt of the invoice.

Either party may terminate this agreement without cause before shipment by giving written notice.

<!-- Clause Type: Termination for Convenience -->

SecureWare AG's liability shall be limited to replacement of defective devices or refund of the purchase price.

<!-- Clause Type: Limitation of Liability -->

The customer agrees not to resell the devices to sanctioned entities.
