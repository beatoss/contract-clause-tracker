# Partnership Agreement

This Partnership Agreement is made between GreenGrid AG and Urban Energy Solutions Ltd.

The parties will jointly develop proposals for renewable energy infrastructure projects.

Either party may withdraw from the partnership for any reason with 30 days written notice.

<!-- Clause Type: Termination for Convenience -->

Neither party shall be liable for consequential damages related to unsuccessful bids.

<!-- Clause Type: Limitation of Liability -->

Each party must keep shared pricing models and technical plans confidential.

The parties will meet monthly to review active opportunities and project risks.
