# Employment Agreement

This Employment Agreement is made between Example Corp and John Doe.

The employee shall receive an annual salary of CHF 120000.

The employee agrees not to work for a competing company within Switzerland for 12 months after termination of employment.

<!-- Clause Type: Non-Compete -->

Either party may terminate the employment relationship with a notice period of 60 days.

<!-- Clause Type: Termination for Convenience -->

The employer shall not be responsible for losses caused by third-party software failures.

<!-- Clause Type: Limitation of Liability -->

All confidential information must remain protected after the end of employment.
